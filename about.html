📘 Our Story

What is SmartFuture?

SmartFuture Career Hub is a digital platform designed to help Kenyan students make informed decisions about their future careers and university courses.

We simplify the KUCCPS placement process by providing accurate course recommendations, cluster point analysis, and university insights — eliminating confusion and guiding students toward successful academic and career paths.

🚀 Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading digital platform for career guidance and university placement support, empowering every student to make confident and informed decisions about their future.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accessible, accurate, and technology-driven career guidance tools that help students:

  • Understand their academic strengths
  • Identify suitable degree programs
  • Navigate the KUCCPS process with ease
