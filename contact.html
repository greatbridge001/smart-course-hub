❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?
We charge only KSh 1 as a verification fee to unlock your personalized course recommendations. That's it — no hidden fees.
How accurate are the recommendations?
Our engine follows official KUCCPS guidelines, cluster subject requirements, and minimum grade rules. Results are highly accurate.
Can I download my results?
Yes! After payment and recommendation generation, you can download a full report as a text file to share with parents or teachers.
Payment failed — what do I do?
Use the "Check Payment Status" button after paying. If issues persist, contact us via WhatsApp at 0769642043 for immediate help.
