💡 Quick Tips

KUCCPS Application Quick Tips

📅
Know the Deadlines
KUCCPS applications open after KCSE results. Monitor the KUCCPS portal and apply as early as possible.
📋
List 6 Choices
KUCCPS allows you to list up to 6 course choices. Always include backup options in case your top choice is competitive.
🧮
Calculate Cluster Points
Each course uses 4 specific subjects. Make sure you calculate cluster points for your target course before applying.
🎯
Match Your Strengths
Choose courses that align with your best subjects. You'll excel more in a field where your strengths are naturally matched.
